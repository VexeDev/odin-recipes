Pasta Salad

Description

Dive into a bowl of vibrant and refreshing pasta salad, a perfect dish for any occasion. This delightful salad combines al dente pasta with a medley of crisp vegetables, all tossed in a zesty homemade dressing. The colorful assortment of cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers not only adds a variety of textures but also a burst of fresh flavors that complement the tender bites of pasta. Finished with a sprinkle of tangy feta cheese and aromatic herbs, this pasta salad is a crowd-pleaser that's as pleasing to the eye as it is to the palate. Ideal for picnics, potlucks, or a nutritious family dinner, this awesome pasta salad is a versatile dish that's both easy to assemble and delicious to devour.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Bell pepper (any color)
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Dijon mustard
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. 1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente, then rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. 2. Halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, thinly slice the red onion, and chop the bell pepper into bite-sized pieces.
  3. 3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled pasta with the prepared vegetables and Kalamata olives.
  4. 4. Crumble feta cheese over the pasta and vegetables, then add chopped fresh parsley.
  5. 5. In a separate small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and black pepper to create the dressing.
  6. 6. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss everything together until well coated.
  7. 7. Chill the pasta salad in the refrigerator before serving to allow the flavors to meld. Enjoy!
