Mini Sliders

Description

Satisfy your cravings with these mouthwatering mini sliders, the ultimate finger food for any gathering or game day. Each little burger is a juicy delight, featuring a perfectly seasoned beef patty that's grilled to tender perfection and nestled between soft, toasted mini buns. Topped with a slice of melty cheese, a dollop of tangy sauce, and a layer of crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, and zesty pickles, these sliders pack a flavorful punch in every bite. They're small enough to pop in your mouth whole but pack enough flavor to please even the heartiest appetite. Whether served as an appetizer or a main dish, these mini sliders are sure to be a big hit!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Mini burger buns
  • Cheese slices (e.g., cheddar or American)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter (for toasting buns)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into small, palm-sized patties, slightly larger than the buns. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the patties on the grill or skillet, cooking for about 2-3 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or until they reach your preferred level of cooking.
  4. A minute before the patties are done, top each with a slice of cheese to melt.
  5. While the patties cook, split the mini buns in half and lightly butter the cut sides. Toast the buns on the grill or in a separate skillet until golden brown.
  6. Prepare the toppings by washing and drying the lettuce, slicing the tomato into thin rounds, and cutting the pickles if needed.
  7. Assemble the sliders by spreading ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the bottom half of the toasted buns, followed by a lettuce leaf, a tomato slice, and a pickle.
  8. Place a cheese-topped beef patty on each prepared bun bottom, then cap with the top half of the buns.
  9. Serve the mini sliders immediately while they're warm and the cheese is still gooey. Enjoy!
